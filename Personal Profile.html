Personal Profile

Myers-Briggs Test

The results from the Myers-Briggs test showed that my personality type was a defender or an ISFJ. These test results mean that I am very kind, loyal and considerate towards others. This also means though that I am a private person with a small friend group. Another characteristic of a defender is that they are organised and plan-ahead. They are also responsible with the time management of tasks, by ensuring all tasks are completed on time. All these characteristics describe how I live my life. The results show that my personality type can be both good and bad for teamwork. This is the case because, while I ensure all my tasks are completed on time, I am also a private person which can mean I will sometimes be reluctant to speak with other members of the group. I am also kind, loyal and considerate which are all good attributes of a good team player.
